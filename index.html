Prove yourself and join the Fremen Army! Offworlders are invading our territories. Our Sietches are threatened. Our fighters are many but we are speard across the desert.

Be warned, we do not accept every volunteer. You will need to prove that you are worth more as a fighter than you are for your bodies' water. Through the Fremen you will learn many skills, or perhaps increase a skill you have already learned. Some of these skills include:

  • Fighting in the Weirding Way
  • Water collection and conservation
  • The Sandwalk

Living in the desert of Arrakis is dangerous. Should you be accepted, we offer a few features to help ease the mind.

  • An alliance with House Atreides. We have a mutual respect between the Fremen and the Atreides.
